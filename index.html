About AFRICAN iT MASTERY

AFRICAN iT MASTERY, is a full six months course in a specific area of choice. We give the opportunity to our students to prefer their form of study (Online or Physical). ONLINE STUDENTS: Learn at your own pace and location with self-paced, online versions technology. NOTE: The prices differs from the online study and physical study. GOOD NEWS TO OUR STUDENTS: We are currently offering SCHOLARSHIP for both online and physical study, meaning you just have to pay something little for the maintenance of the tools and equipment used and you can access a hand full skills and experience with our instructors. IMPORTANT NOTICE: We offer all our students a certificate as a recognition after successful complication and most importantly, offers all the best student an opportunity to grow and expand their business with us.
